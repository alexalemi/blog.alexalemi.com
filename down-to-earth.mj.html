Down to Earth

Alexander A. Alemi. 2025-06-28

Today my son asked me a simple question:

If you dropped a feather from the moon, how long would it take to land on the earth?

I tried to answer the question and discovered that the question was a bit more subtle than I initially thought it would be. It also made me think more about science education and how we teach students to solve problems.

Overall, I feel as though science education is too formulaic and rigid. My experience as a physics TA showed me that even at ivy league schools, students have a tendency to put their science and math education in the upside-down1.

1 Coined for the alternative reality where the monsters lived in Stranger Things.
